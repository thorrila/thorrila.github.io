Taraval's Hidden Crisis

Taraval is a residential district in southwest San Francisco, known for its quiet and safe small-town feel. So why is labor trafficking reported at over 7 times the city average?

The data comes from the San Fransisco Police Departments public incident report database, covering over 20 years. Because labor trafficking is notoriously underreported, these numbers are likely a floor, not a ceiling.

While kidnapping and sex trafficking remain low, labor trafficking (forcing labor through debt or coercion) is where Taraval stands dramatically apart.
