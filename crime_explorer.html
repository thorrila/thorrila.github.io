Interactive Crime Explorer

Explore the crucial difference between Location Quotient ratios and Absolute Counts. Select any crime and toggle views to investigate which hotspots are genuine versus statistical mirages.

How to Read This Tool

  • Location Quotient View: Statistical ratios where > 1 = overrepresented. Watch for high LQ with low counts = mirage.
  • Absolute Counts View: Real incident numbers. Validates whether high LQ ratios are backed by data.
  • Investigation: Pick a crime, check LQ, then switch to counts. Are "hottest" districts just noise?
